Research

Work in Progress

  • Asymptotic Theory for Robust Exponentially Tilted Methods Under Weak Dependence.
    Develops asymptotic theory for robust exponentially tilted estimators in weakly dependent time-series settings.
  • Bootstrap Inference for Robust Moment-Condition Estimators with Dependent Data.
    Proposes dependence-preserving block bootstrap procedures to improve finite-sample inference for robust moment-condition estimators.
  • Nonlinear Difference-in-Differences under Spatio-Temporal Dependence.
    Extends nonlinear Difference-in-Differences to settings with spatio-temporal dependence for credible policy evaluation.

Publications & Articles

  • Analysis of Immigration Patterns Pre and Post Democratic Transition: Evidence from Tunisia. Journal of Development and Economic Policies, 2024.
    Examines immigration patterns in Tunisia before and after the democratic transition using Arab Barometer data, with Probit estimates and a Changes-in-Changes robustness check.
