About

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at Concordia University. My research focuses on developing econometric methods for credible inference under dependence—extending robust estimation and bootstrap procedures for time-series data and studying how spatial dependence affects nonlinear policy evaluation.

You can find details on my work on the Research page.
